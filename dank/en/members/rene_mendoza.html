Dr. René Mendoza Vidaurre

René is a facilitator of ideas and knowledges. His passion is working with associated organizations and communities in Latin America. He makes studies about the experiences of rural organizations wrapped in any initiative, organizes reflexion and learning spaces with them, and contributes to the democratization of knowing and of knowledge, publishing his works.


René has a PhD in Development Studies. Hasw a Masters in Globalization and Development of the University of Antwerp, Belgium and the Jesuits universities of Central America, researcher associated with IOB-University of Antwerp and colaborator of the Wind of Peace Foundation.


Has been director of the Institute of Research and Development, Nitlapan, of the Universidad Centroamericana, Nicaragua (Central American University), coordinator of various research teams, design of development programs, and Diploma organizations.
