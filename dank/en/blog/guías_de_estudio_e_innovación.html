Guides of Studies and Innovation.


There are currently more young people from rural background with superior education than ever before in human history. At the same time, however, peasant and indigenous families with their family agriculture are in their worst ever crisis. The paradox is that this crisis sharpens when they have children, men and women, that have studied. How can we make parents and sons string bridges to reinvent the family agriculture?

An answer is: young boys of age, in mind and spirit, need instruments to become scholars and innovators of their realities. Here are those instruments, 3 guides: How to observe? How to converse? How to take notes and analize them? To obtain them, you need pen and paper, to open your mind to wait for the unexpected, and light that fire you carry inside of you.
